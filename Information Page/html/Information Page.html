Where We Started

Jet Blue Transport & Moving Company, also known as Jet Blue Transport Service, is located at 16 Fatima Trace, Paramin, Maraval 121007. Its founder, Mr. Collin St. Hillaire, registered the company as a sole trader on 7th February 2006, to include the following services: Transport, Tours, Moving Services, Messenger Services and Shuttle Service. At that time, Mr. St. Hillaire had one vehicle, a Nissan Frontier, with which he was able to provide transport services for small businesses such as: House N’ Wares, Maraval; Art and Antique, West Mall; and Austin Chang, Trinicity Industrial Estate.

With the increased demand for house-moving services, he later purchased a 3-ton truck, which gave him the opportunity to do moving jobs for some prestigious and professional clients, including lawyers, such as Justin Phelps; doctors, such as Dr. Brathwaithe; and sporting personalities such as Sunil Narine and Ricardo Powell. Over the years, he provided services for banking institutions such as First Citizens Bank Ltd., AIC Securities Ltd, and JMMB Trinidad and Tobago Group of Companies, where he moved whole offices along with safes.

Safe removal was a specialized service which he offered by outsourcing work to other specialized lift contractors. In addition, he provided services for Ministries including: the Ministry of Finance, the Ministry of National Security, the Central Bank and the Office of the Prime Minister. Finally, his client list includes shipping companies such as Platinum Logistics and the general public, who require packing and moving services to and from the Port of Port of Spain (PPOS), and PLIPDECO.
